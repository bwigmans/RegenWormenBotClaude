# Roasted Worms – Quick Rules

## Goal
Collect the most worms (printed on the helpings) by game end.

## Setup
- Place 16 worm helpings (values 21–36) in ascending order in the center – the **grill**.
- Youngest player takes the 8 dice and starts.

## Turn Structure
1. **Roll all 8 dice**.
2. Choose **one symbol** (1–5 or worm) not yet taken this turn. Take **all dice** showing that symbol.
3. Roll remaining dice, repeat step 2 until:
   - All 8 dice taken,
   - All 6 symbols taken,
   - You stop voluntarily,
   - You roll only symbols already collected (turn fails immediately).

**Worm = 5 points** toward your sum.

## Taking a Worm Helping
When you end your turn (voluntarily or forced):
- If you have **at least one worm** and your total sum matches a **visible** helping:
  - If on grill → take it.
  - If face‑up in front of another player → **snatch** it.
- If the matching helping is your own top card (so not visible) or no match exists:
  - Take the **next lowest** helping from the grill (if any).
- If you overlook a matching helping on another player, take next lowest from grill.

Place each taken helping **face‑up** on top of your stack. Only the top is vulnerable; underneath are safe.

## Unsuccessful Turn
A turn fails if:
- You end with **no worm** collected,
- You cannot take any helping (no match and no lower on grill),
- You rolled only symbols already collected.

**Penalty**: Return your **top** helping to the grill (if you have any). Then turn the **highest** face‑up helping on the grill face‑down (it becomes unavailable for the rest of the game). If the returned helping itself is highest, leave it face‑up.

## Game End
Ends when **no face‑up helpings remain on the grill**.

## Scoring
- Count **worms** on all your helpings. Most wins.
- Tie: higher **number** on a single helping wins.
